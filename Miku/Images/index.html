Hatsune Miku

Who is she?

Hatsune Miku is a Japanese Vocaloid Sofware voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media. She is a singing voice syntheziser and has been featured in over 100,00 songs worldwide. Her name means "Sound From the Future", and although fictional, she performs live concerts as a hologram and is well loved by people all around the world. To fans, she is much more than just a synthetic voice. She is a vessel for creativity and has inspired unlimited artistic expression.
