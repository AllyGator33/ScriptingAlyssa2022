ATTRACTIONS

Not only is this rural town home to over 8,000 satisfied Albertans, but it is also home to what is considered to be the best corn in all of Canada. The reason Taber is the Corn Capital of Canada is because of the vast amount of sunlight in the area that supplies the crops, specifically the corn, with a rich taste and bright yellow colour. With the delicious corn also comes the appropriately titled festival, Cornfest, that takes place in Taber every year. Its facilities and events attract people from other towns to come and celebrate, with great food, rides, live entertainment, and overall family fun. You can really tell how much this town is proud of the corn it produces, and rightfully so, as it is a taste foreigners cannot find anywhere else. That is why, along with all the amusing festivities surrounding it, there is also a giant corn stalk statue right alongside the road to really hammer in the love for this juicy yellow crop. If you have high-energy kids that need a place to play, there are various playgrounds, sports teams, and clubs they can join in Taber to help them release all their built-up energy, along with various other recreational areas. Of course, adults are catered to as well, with various wonderful services around the town such as art centres, bars and casinos, campgrounds, and many additional activities beyond that.

Seasonal events that take place every year help make the town feel livelier, such as Halloween and Christmas decorations and activities. Feeling the snow lightly fall onto you as you listen to a band play Christmas music outdoors is only one of the wonderful events you can experience, for free, during the Christmas season in Taber. If you are someone who can appreciate the little things in life and adores the beauty of the natural world, Taber is a wonderful place to visit.
