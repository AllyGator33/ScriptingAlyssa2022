GEOGRAPHY

Taber is a small town located in Southern Alberta, Canada that is surrounded by the Municipal District of Taber. The terrain surrounding Taber is mostly flat, which allows for exceptionally beautiful and clear sunsets. It is a very safe environment and although it is a small town, within it holds big possibilities for a great stay! If you want to settle down for awhile and take a break from bustling crowds and bright city lights, this rural community is a wonderful place to visit. The town is divided by beautiful rolling coulees, as well as lakes and rivers, the perfect spots for fishing.

The nature within and surrounding Taber is serene and alluring, and while it is mostly flat land, there is still so much beauty in even its simplest of terrains. Wildlife is abundant, as you will often see deer prancing alongside the road, beavers swimming happily in rivers, and hawks flying above, making any visit a little more enjoyable.
